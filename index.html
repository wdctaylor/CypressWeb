Our Story

A name chosen for endurance. A foundation built to last. A partner committed to carrying your brand through every storm — and beyond.

The Name

I chose the name Cypress Wood Advertising because of its profound connection to resilience. In the biblical account of the Great Flood, Noah was instructed to build the Ark from gopher wood—which many scholars identify as cypress. It was selected for a specific reason: cypress is one of the few materials on Earth naturally resistant to rot, decay, and water damage.

The Foundation

While other woods would have given way under the pressure of a forty-day storm, cypress remained stable, durable, and buoyant. It was chosen not for its appearance, but for what it could endure. That principle is at the heart of everything we do.

The Partnership

This is exactly how I view my partnership with your organization. The marketing landscape can often feel like a flood—shifting trends, unpredictable algorithms, and relentless noise. My goal is to be the cypress for your brand: a stable, durable foundation that doesn't just weather the storm, but carries you safely toward your goals.

The Promise

At Cypress Wood, we build marketing strategies designed for longevity, integrity, and full-circle success—work that endures long after the initial campaign has ended.
